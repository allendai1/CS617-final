Nantucket has an extremely high median listing price despite not having the most Airbnb listings.
Barnstable has by far the most amount of Airbnb listings with 9136, which makes sense as that area is considered Cape Cod and has a high tourist population, but yet it hasn't been affected by an increase of median listing price.
The county with the second highest amount of listings is Suffolk county, which is the metropolitan area where a majority of the population in Massachusetts live.
Dukes county is the second highest county for price per square foot over time, with some overlap with Suffolk from 2017 and 2020. Dukes is similar to Nantucket in that they're both islands off of Massachusetts, but the population of Dukes county is only 20,600 as of 2020 making it the second-least populous county in Massachusetts. Despite having such small population, it's price increase has almost skyrocketed the past few years.
Franklin county continues to have the least amount of Airbnb listings while also having the smallest median listing price. Despite this, from 07/2016 to 07/2023 it's listing price has gone from $213,000 to $349,000, an increase of 61%.
